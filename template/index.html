ABOUT
BANDIO

Founded in 1999, Bandio is a unique blend of metalcore,
symphonic rock, and electronic music.

What eventually became an award-winning metal band, started as a free-time project back in 1999 by four friends. Harry Peters & John Moore teamed up with their college friends Sam Adams and Edward Emerson to make music that will break all known boundaries years after.
